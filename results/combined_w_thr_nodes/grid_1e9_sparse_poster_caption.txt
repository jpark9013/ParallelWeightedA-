Dataset: Sparse grid n=10^9 (4-neigh, no obstacles) · goal opposite corner
MPI ranks = nodes (1, 2); efficiency uses wall time at nodes=1 as T₁ per series.

w=1 vs w=5 wall times differ by ~1e4 because the search work differs: this TSV reports ~1e9 state expansions for w=1 vs ~7e4–8e4 for w=5 (weighted A* with larger w is more goal-directed, not a plotting error).
Sparse grid — very low w=5 times (~0.02–0.04 s) are real: weighted A* expands on the order of 8e4 states (see TSV expansions column), not a crash.
w=1 — two-node runs use 2 MPI ranks on one node; this placement hit std::bad_alloc for some configs (see logs / omit from chart if missing).

Figures: *_poster_time_faceted.jpg uses separate linear y axes from 0 s per w panel. *_poster_efficiency.jpg shows η = T₁/(N·T_N) with η = 1 as ideal strong scaling.
